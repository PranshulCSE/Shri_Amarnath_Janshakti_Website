History

Our Story

Shri Amarnath JanShakti Sewa Mandal (SAJSSM) is a society dedicated to social service,Langar Sewa, Medical Aid Facility and residencial services to Shri Amarnath Ji Yatris.The following timeline highlights key moments of our Sewa and growth of the organization.

Timeline & Milestones

Founding

Establishment and Vision

Our Ex-President Late Mr. Mukesh Dua Ji along with Some Family Friends founded Shri Amarnath JanShakti Sewa Mandal with the mission to provide Langar Sewa and other Social works like Creamation of Dead Bodies,Blood Donations, selfless services etc.

Begining in 2011

Sewa At Baltal

Initially We Setup 4-5 Camping Tents at Baltal to provide various Services to Yatris.

New Begining in 2023

With the Blessings of Baba Barfani Ji, in May 2023 we got our own permission to setup Langar at Domail,Baltal by Deputy Commissioner of Dist. Ganderbal. After that we provided Service to Yatris in the Year 2023,2024 Selflessly.

Langar At Brari-Marg

Another Key Achievment

In the Year 2025, Shri Amarnath Ji Shrine Board Offers Shri Amarnath JanShakti Sewa Mandal to setup Langar at Brarimarg (Which is the Highest Point of Shri_Amarnath_Ji_Yatra).After that we recieve a Certificate of Appreciation from Honurable Lieutinant Governer of J&K & Chairman Of Shri Amarnath Ji Shrine Board Manoj Sinha Ji.

Today

Planning & Future Plans

Today SAJSSM continues to enhance their services for Next Yatra 2026 and eagerly waiting to serve for humanity in Next Yatra Too.
